## At a Glance

AuthManager is a Paper/Purpur authentication plugin designed to secure offline/premium mixed environments.

Main goals:

- Protect premium usernames from impersonation
- Enforce login/register before gameplay actions
- Reduce brute-force attempts with rate limiting and account locks
- Provide operational tools for administrators
